Welcome to **The Middle Way in AI**.

This is not another AI hype blog. This is not a doom-and-gloom prophecy about the end of software.

This is a resource for builders, investors, and strategists who understand that we're in the middle of a **re-platforming event**—and who want to navigate it intelligently.

## What This Blog Is About

Most discussions about AI fall into two camps:

* **The Utopians** who see AI as a magic solution to every problem
* **The Doomers** who predict the collapse of the entire software economy

I believe the truth lies in the middle. We're not witnessing the death of software—we're witnessing its **evolution from workflows to utility**.

## What You'll Find Here

On this blog, I'll be analyzing the 2026 tech landscape through a specific lens:

* **The Stewardship Test:** Does the technology control a "Physical Anchor" that an LLM cannot bypass?
* **The Agency Test:** Is it a dashboard-first relic, or a seamless database accessible by agents?
* **The Friction Test:** Is revenue tied to human seats (Structural Debt) or to outcomes?

Companies that pass these tests will thrive. Those that don't will struggle.

## Who This Is For

This blog is for you if:

* You're a CTO trying to understand what AI means for your infrastructure
* You're an investor evaluating which companies will survive the shift
* You're a founder building in this new paradigm
* You're a strategist who wants to cut through the noise

## Stop Watching the Brain. Start Watching the Pipes.

The "Frontier Brain" (LLMs) is rapidly becoming a commodity. The real value lies in the **Billion-Dollar Backend**—the physical and data infrastructure that AI depends on but cannot replace.

That's what we'll be exploring here.

Welcome to The Middle Way.
