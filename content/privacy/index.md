*Last updated: March 21, 2026*

This Privacy Policy describes how Crown Point Advisory Group LLC ("CPAG," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with your use of all websites, publications, and digital properties we operate, including but not limited to crownpointadvisorygroup.com, middlewayinai.com, middlewayinai.substack.com, and bridgingtheabyss.substack.com (collectively, the "Properties").

### Section 1: Information We Collect

**1.1 Information You Provide Directly**

We may collect information you voluntarily provide when you submit an inquiry through a contact or intake form, subscribe to a publication through Substack, submit comments or feedback to any Property, or correspond with us by email. This information may include your name, email address, company name, professional title, and any other details you choose to include.

**1.2 Information Collected Automatically**

When you visit the Properties, certain information may be collected automatically through standard web technologies, including IP address and approximate geographic location, browser type and version, device type and operating system, pages visited and time spent on those pages, and referring URLs. This information is collected through analytics tools including Google Analytics and is used in aggregate form to understand how readers engage with the Properties.

**1.3 Information Collected Through Third-Party Platforms**

Certain Properties are hosted on or distributed through Substack. When you subscribe to a Substack publication operated by Crown Point Advisory Group LLC, Substack collects and processes your information in accordance with Substack's own Privacy Policy, available at substack.com/privacy. Crown Point Advisory Group LLC has access to subscriber email addresses and basic engagement data provided by Substack but does not have access to payment information, which is handled entirely by Substack.

### Section 2: How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to respond to inquiries and communicate with readers and subscribers, deliver email newsletters and publication updates, understand how content is being read and engaged with, improve the quality and relevance of content, and fulfill any legal or compliance obligations. We do not use your information for automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects.

### Section 3: AI Tools and Content Development

Crown Point Advisory Group LLC uses artificial intelligence tools to assist in the research, drafting, and development of content published across the Properties. These tools may process text inputs provided by the author in the course of content development. We do not input subscriber personal information, reader data, or any identifiable user data into AI tools. AI tools are used solely for content creation purposes under the editorial direction and review of a human author.

### Section 4: How We Share Your Information

Crown Point Advisory Group LLC does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. We may share information with service providers including Substack, Google Analytics, Netlify, and GitHub in the operation of the Properties. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Crown Point Advisory Group LLC, our readers, or others.

### Section 5: Data Retention

We retain subscriber email addresses and related information for as long as you remain a subscriber to any Property. You may unsubscribe at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email communication. Inquiry and contact form submissions are retained for a reasonable period necessary to respond to and document the communication.

### Section 6: Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, and to opt out of email communications at any time. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information in Section 9 below. California residents may have additional rights under the CCPA. Crown Point Advisory Group LLC does not sell personal information. European residents may have additional rights under the GDPR.

### Section 7: Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The Properties may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to support analytics and improve reader experience. You may configure your browser to refuse cookies. Google Analytics is used across the Properties to collect aggregated, anonymized data about reader behavior. You may opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on available at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

### Section 8: Children's Privacy

The Properties are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

### Section 9: Changes to This Privacy Policy

Crown Point Advisory Group LLC reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. The date of the most recent revision will be noted at the top of this document. Continued use of any Property following the posting of a revised Privacy Policy constitutes acceptance of those changes.

### Section 10: Contact

Questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy may be directed to Crown Point Advisory Group LLC via the contact form at [crownpointadvisorygroup.com](https://crownpointadvisorygroup.com).
