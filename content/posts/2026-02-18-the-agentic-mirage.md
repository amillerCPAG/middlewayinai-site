Utopians promise a world where "Claude Cowork" agents handle your entire back office by next Tuesday.

Doomers promise those same agents will hallucinate your company into bankruptcy.

**The Middle Lane Pivot:** The real bottleneck isn't the agent's intelligence; it's the Billion-Dollar Backend.

**The Reality:** Everyone wants autonomous agents, but nobody's data is ready for them. We are currently watching the "Infrastructure Gap" widen. You can't deploy an agentic workforce on top of a fragmented, un-auditable data swamp.

**The Solution:** The winners in 2026 won't be the ones with the "smartest" agents—they’ll be the ones who focus on Frictionless Integration. If your data foundation isn't deterministic, your agents are just expensive toys.
